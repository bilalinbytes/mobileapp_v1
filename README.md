# Saans Sync

# webversionSS

Saans Sync is a respiratory care platform designed to keep doctors and patients connected between visits through daily tracking, organized clinical review, and timely follow-up.

## What Saans Sync Does

Saans Sync supports long-term respiratory care by turning everyday patient updates into actionable clinical insights.

- Gives patients a structured way to log day-to-day respiratory status
- Gives doctors a centralized view of patient status, progress, and risk signals
- Improves continuity of care by reducing gaps between appointments

## Core Features

- Role-based experiences for doctors and patients
- Patient onboarding and profile capture for care coordination
- Daily symptom and status logging for respiratory conditions
- Clinical history and trend views to support follow-up decisions
- Care workflows tailored to asthma, COPD, bronchiectasis, ILD/IPF, and post-ICU recovery
- Export-oriented workflows for reporting and handoff needs

## Who It Is For

- Pulmonologists and respiratory care teams who need a clear view of patient progress
- Patients who need a simple routine to report symptoms and recovery signals
- Care settings where regular monitoring helps enable earlier intervention

## Why It Matters

- Reduces missed warning signs by organizing day-to-day updates into a clinical view
- Supports better doctor-patient alignment with shared context on current status
- Helps teams make faster, better-informed follow-up decisions
